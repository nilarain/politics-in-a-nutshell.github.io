Election Timeline

  • April 12, 2015

    Hillary Clinton announces she is running for president.

  • June 16, 2015

    Donald Trump officially enters the presidential race as well.

  • July 18-21, 2016

    The Republican National Convention gathers in Cleveland, Ohio to announce their platform and official candidate, Donald Trump, on the final day.

  • July 20, 2016

    Ted Cruz is booed at the RNC for refusing to endorse Trump's nomination, ending a bitter rivalry from the Republican primaries.

  • July 24, 2016

    DNC chair Debbie Wasserman Schultz resigns after an email leak that revealed the DNC's bias against Bernie Sanders and raised questions about party unity.

  • July 25-28, 2016

    The Democratic National Convention congregates in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. They nominate Clinton as the first ever female presidential nominee.

  • UPCOMING

    The first presidential debate will occur on September 26 at Hofsta University in Hemstead, NY.
